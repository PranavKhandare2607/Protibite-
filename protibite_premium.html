The Cream — What Makes It Real

🍋

100% Real Fruit Purée Base

No artificial flavour concentrates. Each variant uses actual fruit pulp — mango, chikoo, guava or strawberry — processed to retain natural taste, colour and nutrients.

💧

High Moisture, Flowing Texture

Unlike dry or pasty cream biscuits, ProtiBite's cream is formulated with higher moisture content — making it liquid-like, glossy and explosively flavourful when you bite in.

🚫

No Artificial Colours or Preservatives

The vibrant orange of mango, the deep red of strawberry — all from the fruit itself. Zero synthetic dyes. Zero preservatives. Just real goodness.

⚖️

Balanced Sweetness with Natural Sugars

No refined sugar overload. The cream's sweetness comes from dates, natural fruit sugars and a carefully controlled formula — indulgent yet mindful.

🌡️

Stable Under Every Condition

Specially formulated cream that stays lush and pourable even after baking and packaging — maintaining its signature soft, melting quality until the very last bite.
